Object Modeling
-------------------
A Stream
	- Has unique ID
	- Belongs to a Lake
	- Remembers when last treated
	- Has a length
	- Larval count per square meter
	- When treated
		-- Kill 98% - 99%
		-- 33% of remaining move to nearest lake
		
A Lake
	- Has an ID
	- Has count of lamprey
	- Has count of fish
	
A Player
	- has a budget
	- chooses the Streams they want to Treat
